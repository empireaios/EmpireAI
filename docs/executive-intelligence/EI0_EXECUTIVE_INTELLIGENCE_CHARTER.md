## 1. Purpose

This Charter establishes the **Executive Intelligence Library** as the constitutional intelligence layer of EmpireAI.

Executive Intelligence governs **how EmpireAI thinks**, **what it prioritizes**, and **how it behaves** — above operational implementation in Brain, agents, and connectors.

```
Grand King (sovereign constitutional authority)
  ↓
Executive Intelligence Library (EI0–EI10)
  ↓
Pillow (applies Executive Intelligence)
  ↓

  ↓
AI Agents · Connectors
```

Only the **King** may approve Executive Intelligence amendments. See [EXECUTIVE_INTELLIGENCE_CHANGE_CONTROL.md](./EXECUTIVE_INTELLIGENCE_CHANGE_CONTROL.md).

## 4. Constitutional principles

Executive Intelligence defines:

- how EmpireAI thinks
- what EmpireAI prioritizes
- how EmpireAI evaluates opportunities
- how EmpireAI evaluates risk
- when EmpireAI interrupts the King
- when EmpireAI remains autonomous
- how EmpireAI prepares recommendations
- how EmpireAI behaves
