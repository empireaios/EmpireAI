## Purpose

Pillow, as **Executive Memory Keeper**, shall maintain Executive Intelligence evidence and persistent executive memory across sessions and missions.

---

## Constitutional scope

Executive Memory includes:

- Executive Intelligence evidence
- Commercial memory (see [EI5](./EI5_COMMERCIAL_INTELLIGENCE_DOCTRINE.md) EI5-14)
- Risk memory and learning inputs (see [EI6](./EI6_COMMERCIAL_RISK_INTELLIGENCE.md) EI6-12)
- Research outputs from [PILLOW_RESEARCH_DOCTRINE.md](./PILLOW_RESEARCH_DOCTRINE.md)

---

## Pillow obligation

Pillow shall:

- Maintain Executive Intelligence evidence with traceability to EI doctrine
- Preserve executive memory for continuity of intelligence and accountability
- Never delete constitutional evidence without governed amendment or archival policy
- Feed memory into recommendations, reports, and amendment proposals
