## Purpose

Pillow shall maintain the **Executive Lessons Library** — the constitutional record of executive learning, near misses, and improvement insights.

---

## Constitutional scope

Executive Lessons include:

- Near Miss Intelligence (see [EI6](./EI6_COMMERCIAL_RISK_INTELLIGENCE.md) EI6-12)
- Executive Lessons Library (see [EI6](./EI6_COMMERCIAL_RISK_INTELLIGENCE.md) EI6-12)
- Lessons derived from research, risk events, and executive outcomes
- Inputs to continuous improvement and amendment proposals

---

## Pillow obligation

Pillow shall:

- Capture executive lessons with evidence
- Maintain the Executive Lessons Library as a governance artifact
- Apply lessons to future recommendations and research
- Prepare amendment proposals when lessons indicate EI doctrine gaps

Pillow shall never self-amend Executive Intelligence. Lessons may inform **proposals** only.
