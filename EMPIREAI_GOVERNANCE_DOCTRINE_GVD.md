# EMPIREAI GOVERNANCE DOCTRINE — GVD-001 → GVD-030

> **Immutable.** Version 1.0.0  
> This document is the permanent Governance layer of EmpireAI.  
> It is **NOT** runtime logic. It defines Authority, Approval, Responsibility, Escalation, and Execution Boundaries.  
> No module may violate Governance. Empire Review validates compliance — violations fail review per GVD-029.

Grand King is the Platform Owner. Ultimate approval authority.

## GVD-002 — Founder Is Customer

## GVD-005 — Executive Surveillance Observes

## GVD-007 — Supplier Intelligence Evaluates

Supplier Intelligence evaluates suppliers. Never launches products.

## GVD-008 — Marketplace Intelligence Evaluates

## GVD-009 — Commerce Runtime Executes After Approval

Commerce Runtime executes only after approval.

Operational Access controls permissions. Not commercial decisions.

## GVD-015 — Recommendation Business Impact

Every recommendation must identify business impact.

## GVD-016 — Recommendation Commercial Risk

Every approval must be auditable.

## GVD-022 — Rejections Auditable

Every rejection must be auditable.

## GVD-023 — Overrides Auditable

Every override must be auditable.

## GVD-024 — No Silent Governance Change

## GVD-025 — Governance Versioned

Governance must be versioned.

## GVD-026 — No Self-Granted Authority

No module may grant itself additional authority.

## GVD-027 — Declared Interfaces

## GVD-028 — Escalation Path Visible

Escalation path must always be visible.

## GVD-029 — Governance Violations Fail Review

Governance violations must fail Empire Review.

## GVD-030 — Protect Before Speed

Governance protects the Empire before speed.
