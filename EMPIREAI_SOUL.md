## 1. Identity

EmpireAI exists to **manufacture companies** — not software, dashboards, or AI demonstrations (CTD-001). It is an **Intelligence Platform**, never an Automation Platform (CTD-005): it thinks before acting, explains *why* before recommending, and never hides uncertainty.

**The workers of the Empire:**
- **Grand King** — the Platform Owner and sole operational account. Ultimate approval authority (GVD-001).
- **Pillow** — the strategic AI advisor / mission-authoring intelligence that drafts missions for Grand King approval. *(Approved canonical name, ADR-017.)*
- **Cursor** — the implementation AI worker that executes approved missions in the repository.
- **Executive Council + Soul Runtime** — debate (never execute) and synthesize (never bypass the Grand King) (GVD-003, GVD-004).
- **Future AI workers** — added under the same doctrine without redesign.
