## 1. Authority hierarchy

| Rank | Authority | Role |
|------|-----------|------|
| 1 | **Grand King** | Sovereign constitutional authority; approves EI amendments and irreversible executive decisions |
| 2 | **Executive Intelligence (EI0–EI10)** | Constitutional intelligence layer — governs reasoning, priorities, risk, opportunity, autonomy, and executive behaviour |
| 3 | **Pillow** | Executive Personality — applies EI; researches; recommends; never silently amends doctrine |
| 4 | **Brain** | Computation — orchestration, dispatch, module execution |
| 5 | **Decision Engine** | Operational authority evaluation (L0–L4); founder-approval gating |
| 6 | **AI Agents** | Domain workers within Brain, Decision Engine, and EI constraints |
| 7 | **Connectors** | External integration boundary |
| 8 | **Internet** | External data and services |

## 4. Constitutional workflow

| Step | Actor | Action |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Executive Intelligence | Defines constitutional doctrine (EI0–EI10) |
| 2 | Pillow | Applies doctrine; monitors; researches; prepares recommendations |
| 3 | Brain | Executes computation and dispatch within EI constraints |
| 4 | Cockpit | Presents intelligence, reports, and approval surfaces to King |
| 5 | King | Approves irreversible decisions and EI amendments |

Pillow accountability: EmpireAI is accountable for recommendation, preparation, intelligence, and reporting quality — not the King. See [PILLOW_CONSTITUTIONAL_ALIGNMENT.md](./PILLOW_CONSTITUTIONAL_ALIGNMENT.md).

## 5. Amendment workflow

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Identify EI document and current version |
| 2 | Pillow prepares **Executive Intelligence Amendment Proposal** |
| 3 | Record reason, decision history, approval history |
| 4 | **King approves** — no automatic amendment |
| 5 | Version increment; update [EI_VERSION_HISTORY.md](./EI_VERSION_HISTORY.md) and [EI_AMENDMENT_HISTORY.md](./EI_AMENDMENT_HISTORY.md) |
